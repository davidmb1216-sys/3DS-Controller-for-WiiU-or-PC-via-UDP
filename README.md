# 3DS-Controller-for-WiiU-or-PC-via-UDP
This Repository is a public distribution of an early aplication that enables a connection between your 3DS and your PC or your WIIU, still is in development stage and has a lot of work left to do but , it uses your wifi to conect via UDP your 3DS to anything, just modify the IP on your main.c file to your IPv4. BOTH DEVICES MUST BE ON THE SAME WIFI
